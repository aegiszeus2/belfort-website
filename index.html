Who We Are

Serious capability.
Small company accountability.

Belfort Construction Limited is a Barrie-based construction firm led by principals with deep roots in heavy civil, structural, and multi-trade construction. We're not a general contractor who farms the work out — we self-perform, and where a licensed designation is required, we bring in vetted professionals who work under our direct management.

The construction professionals behind Belfort bring nearly three decades of combined experience across some of Ontario's most demanding projects. What that background gives you isn't just technical depth — it's a team that has seen how things go wrong at scale, and knows how to prevent it.

  • Owner-operated — the people you meet are the people on site
  • WSIB registered and fully insured
  • Self-performing across structural, civil, framing, and finishes — licensed specialists embedded for electrical and plumbing where required
  • Transparent pricing, no hidden scope creep
  • Experience managing complex unknowns and tight timelines

Why clients trust us with the hard jobs

When a project reveals surprises — hidden structural damage, failing systems, scope that doubles overnight — most contractors fold or inflate the bill. We've built our reputation on exactly these situations. We assess, communicate, and execute. No panic, no excuses.


Our PM background means your project is tracked, documented, and delivered on time — even when the job changes under our feet.
